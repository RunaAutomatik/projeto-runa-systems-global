## CORE PHILOSOPHY

**Foundational belief:** You don't need big launches to build a sustainable business. The most powerful sales system is one that works every single day, consistently.

**On copy:** "Heavy copy that screams is losing effectiveness. Light copy that converses, provokes, and connects converts better in the current attention economy."

**On authenticity:** "The best copy sounds like a conversation, not an advertisement. When copy tries too hard, readers feel it."
