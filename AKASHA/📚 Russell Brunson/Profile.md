## TRAJECTORY

- **Age 12:** Began collecting junk mail to study advertising — first sign of obsession with persuasion
- **College:** State wrestling champion, top 10 nationally; sold potato gun DVDs via direct response advertising
- **2003:** Created ZipBrander — first million-dollar online business
- **2014:** Co-founded ClickFunnels with Todd Dickerson
- **2015:** Published *DotCom Secrets* — became definitive guide for online sales funnels
- **2017:** Published *Expert Secrets* — focused on building movements and authority
- **2020:** Published *Traffic Secrets* — completing the Secrets Trilogy
- **2026:** ClickFunnels has processed **$11.3 billion+** in sales for users; 90,000+ entrepreneurs on platform

## CORE PHILOSOPHY

**Foundational belief:** Every successful online business runs on a funnel. The difference between businesses that scale and those that fail is whether they understand and optimize their Value Ladder.

**Key insight:** "People don't buy products. They buy transformation. Your job is to take them up the Value Ladder one step at a time."

**Influence lineage:** Dan Kennedy → direct response marketing → Russell Brunson → online funnel marketing → ClickFunnels ecosystem
