# Site de Movimento — Master Briefing

> Source of truth for LP build, copy, offer, and future Protocolo Sites product.

## Product Definition

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Working name | Site de Movimento |
| Type | Scroll-driven video website (GSAP + canvas frame rendering) |
| Format | Institutional sites + Landing Pages |
| Sections | 4–5 max |
| Complexity | No DB, no complex backend |
| Delivery SLA | 24–48h after contract |
| Includes | Hosting + domain (embedded in price) |
| Excludes | Maintenance (charged separately on demand) |
| Target volume | 15 clients/month |

## Pricing & Offer

| Tier | Price | Condition |
|---|---|---|
| Launch | R$ 1.000 | First 10 clients only |
| Standard | R$ 1.500 | After launch batch |

- Single product, no plans/tiers
- No maintenance included in base price
- Offer structure (bonuses, guarantee, scarcity) → **ARES** to define

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** SMBs (PMEs) across Brazil — no website yet
- **Secondary:** SMBs with outdated/poor quality website
- **Prospecting method:** Daily Google Maps scraping (big cities first → expand nationwide)
- **Outreach channels:** WhatsApp, email, direct call (outbound)

### Pain Points
- Hired freelancers → poor result
- Hired cheap agencies → promises not delivered
- Some paid R$2k–4k to agencies → still got a bad site
- Result they want: more clients, more conversion, more credibility

## Differentiation

- Scroll-driven video animation — scarce in Brazilian SMB market
- Blue ocean positioning
- Speed: 48h delivery vs. weeks from agencies
- Focused on service businesses (not product catalogs)
- Can deliver promotional LPs for specific products/offers

## Funnel & Tech Stack

- **LP:** Stripe payment link embedded (direct purchase)
- **CTA options:** Direct payment OR schedule call
- **Outbound:** Google Maps scraping → WhatsApp/email/call
- **Visual direction:** Premium, minimalist, elegant, impactful
- **Logo:** Exists (visual identity extracted from video)
