```

one hand lightly raised near chest in a relaxed gesture.

- [DESCREVA PEÇA 1: tipo, cor, tecido, modelagem]
```

## PROMPT DE VÍDEO — Sora 2

### V-01 — Story natural (15s)

```
A woman speaking naturally to the camera as if recording a casual Instagram story.
She speaks with calm confidence, making small natural hand gestures.
The camera is held slightly above eye level, selfie style.
Soft indoor lighting. Background is a simple room.
Movement is fluid and organic — not rehearsed.
No text on screen. No cuts. Single continuous take.
```

---

### V-02 — Falando enquanto anda

```
A woman walking casually in an indoor corridor or near a window,
glancing at the camera occasionally while speaking softly.
Natural steps, relaxed arms, ambient indoor light.
Selfie-style framing, slightly unstable as if handheld.
Short, candid, real moment feel.
```

---

### V-03 — Reação / expressão estática

```
A woman reacting with a quiet smile and subtle nod,
as if hearing something that resonates with her.
No movement beyond the natural expression.
Soft light, indoor setting, slight warmth.
15 seconds of authentic stillness with micro-expressions.
```

## Como usar estes prompts

1. Identifique a categoria que corresponde ao conteúdo que você precisa
2. Substitua os placeholders `[TOM DE PELE]`, `[DESCREVA ROUPA]` pelos dados do seu Storyboard
3. Use o agente LENS para otimizar antes de gerar
4. Para prompts de vídeo, use o agente REEL para adaptar ao Sora 2
