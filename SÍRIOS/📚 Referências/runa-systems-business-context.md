## Business Model: Two Pillars

### Pillar 1 — Knowledge Sales (PRIMARY)

Productized knowledge. No founder time in deliverables.

**Product web (teia de produtos):**
- 5–7 products per year
- Price range: R$97 – R$3,997
- Logic: each product solves one problem → creates desire for next product
- Goal: keep customer in perpetual buying state
- Each product attracts different customer profiles
- What one product doesn't reach, another does → base population strategy

**High-ticket products:**
| Product | Format | Price |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Mentorship / Consulting / Intervention | Founder presence in client business (X days) + 90-day async support + 6-month recorded access | R$30k–R$50k |
| RUNA Program | Full program recorded, no founder presence | R$15k |

**Intervention model (high-ticket):**
- Founder enters client business for defined period
- Identifies bottlenecks, suggests optimizations, implements solutions
- Shows construction in practice directly in client's ecosystem
- After direct presence period: client walks independently
- Async support continues for ~90 days
- Recorded material access for ~6 months

### Pillar 2 — Service Sales (SECONDARY)

Done-for-you. Client wants solution built and maintained.

**Services:**
- Websites
- Software / dashboards / systems
- AI squads and agents (custom implementation)

## Customer Journey Logic

```
Low ticket product (R$97–R$997)
    → solves problem A
    → surfaces problem B
    → leads to mid ticket (R$997–R$3,997)
        → solves problem B
        → surfaces problem C
        → leads to RUNA Program (R$15k)
            → or direct to Intervention (R$30k–R$50k)
```

Different entry products attract different profiles.
All roads eventually lead to high ticket.
